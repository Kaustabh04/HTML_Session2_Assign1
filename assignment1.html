Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of the favorite foods. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrabled eggs.

Ingrediants

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in the bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a scapula fold the egg from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moists remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
